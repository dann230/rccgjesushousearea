About Us

Growing in Christ, Impacting Lives

RCCG Jesus House Area is a family of faith committed to helping people grow in Christ and fulfill God’s purpose for their lives. As part of The Redeemed Christian Church of God, we are passionate about worship, prayer, discipleship, and sharing the love of Christ with our community.

We are committed to the vision of RCCG: to make heaven and take as many people as possible with us, through the preaching of the Gospel, holy living, and impacting lives for Christ.
